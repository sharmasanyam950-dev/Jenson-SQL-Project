## ğŸ“Š Business Questions Solved
This project answers the following analytical questions:

2. Calculate the cumulative quantity sold for each product over time.
3. Identify the product with the highest total sales in each category.
4. Find the customer who spent the most money on orders.
5. Determine the highest-priced product in each category.
6. Calculate the total number of orders placed by each customer per store.
7. Identify staff members who have not made any sales.

9. Calculate the median value of the product price list.
10. List products that have never been ordered.
11. Identify staff members who performed above the average sales level.
12. Find customers who ordered products from all available categories.
